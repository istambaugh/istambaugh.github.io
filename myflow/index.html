My Flow

My flow is lifting weights. It is something that I have really come to enjoy, and it is probably my largest hobby.

Why?

I lifted for the first time in middle school. However, I did not get really into it until around the beginning of Covid. With a lot of extra time on my hands, I began to learn a lot more about it, with the largest being the importance of nutrition. Lifting quickly became a part of my daily routine, and it is what I look forward to most everyday.
